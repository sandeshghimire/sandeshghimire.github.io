About

I am an embedded software engineer working at a stealth mode health care startup at Silicon Valley. I have over a decade of experience working on mission critical embedded systems from automotive system, networking time and synchronization, consumer electronic and medical device.

I have significant experience working with ARM M0, M3 and M4 microcontrollers from wide variety of venders including NXP, Freescale, Atmel, ST Micro, TI and Microchip.

For the last several years I have been working on ARM application processors from wide variety of vendors including Xilinx, Altera, TI, Broadcom and qualcomm . I have single handedly build the board support package, port bootloader, port and configure Linux kernel, write device drivers from scratch and developed applications for wide variety of products.

I have worked on many proprietary boards during my career which i am not in liberty to publish, however here are some of the open source sample projects i can share with you. I hope this website help you quickly get started with your development process.
