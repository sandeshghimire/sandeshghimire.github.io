About

I am an embedded firmware engineer working at a stealth mode health care startup at Silicon Valley. I have over a decade of experience working on mission critical embedded systems from automotive system, networking time and synchronization, consumer electronic and medical device.

I have significant experience working with ARM M0, M3 and M4 microcontrollers from wide verity of venders including NXP, Freescale, Atmel, ST Micro, TI and Microchip.

For the last few years I have been working on ARM A9/A15 SoC from Xilinx and Altera. I have single handedly build the board support package, port bootloader (u-boot). Port Linux kernel, write device drivers and applications
